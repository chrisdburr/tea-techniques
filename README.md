# TEA Techniques

![An illustration showing different techniques for assurance](https://alan-turing-institute.github.io/turing-commons/assets/images/illustrations/trust-yellow.png)

[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-blue.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT)

**TEA Techniques** is an interactive database designed to help practitioners identify and implement appropriate assurance methods for responsible AI design, development, and deployment.

It serves as a core plugin for the [Trustworthy and Ethical Assurance (TEA) platform](https://assuranceplatform.azurewebsites.net/), providing a curated collection of techniques to evidence claims about your AI systems.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 🔍 Interactive Technique Finder
Not sure where to start? Our **Technique Finder Wizard** guides you to the right tools.
- **Tailored Recommendations**: Answer targeted questions about your project to get specific suggestions.
- **Flexible Entry Points**: Start based on your **Assurance Goal**, **Model Type**, or **Technique Type**.
- **Smart Filtering**: The wizard adapts to your answers, hiding irrelevant options and showing you exactly why a technique matches your needs.

### 📚 Comprehensive Catalogue
Explore a detailed collection of **> 100 responsible AI techniques** organized by **7 Assurance Goals**:
- **Explainability**
- **Fairness**
- **Privacy**
- **Reliability**
- **Safety**
- **Security**
- **Transparency**

### 🏷️ Advanced Filtering
Browse the database using our powerful tagging system. Filter techniques by:
- **Expertise Level** (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
- **Lifecycle Stage** (Design, Development, Deployment, etc.)
- **Applicable Models** (LLMs, Neural Networks, etc.)
- And more...

## 🚀 Getting Started

Visit the live site to start exploring:
**[https://alan-turing-institute.github.io/tea-techniques](https://alan-turing-institute.github.io/tea-techniques)**

### How to Use
1.  **Browse by Category**: If you know the assurance goal you're targeting (e.g., Fairness), navigate directly to that category to see all relevant techniques.
2.  **Use the Wizard**: Click "Find the Right Technique" to let our guided tool help you discover methods based on your specific project context.
3.  **Search & Filter**: Use the search bar or sidebar filters to narrow down techniques by specific criteria like model type or required expertise.
